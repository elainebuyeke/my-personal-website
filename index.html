Aqua Strides Academy

Club information

Looking for a fun, professional and inclusive swimming club? Aqua Strides Academy is the perfect place for swimmers of all ages and skill levels! Whether you are a beginner learning the basics or a competitive swimmer aiming to improve your technique, our expert coaches are here to guide you. We are proud to have Michael Phelps, a former Olympic gold medalist and swimmer, on our coaching team! With years of elite-level experience, they bring world-class techniques, training strategies and valuable insights to help you reach your full potential in the water.

At Aqua Strides Academy, we believe in creating a positive and encouraging environment. Our coaches are not only highly skilled but also supportive and approachable, ensuring every swimmer feels comfortable, motivated and confident.Plus, our state-of-the-art heated pool allows for year-round training in a comfortable setting. No matter the season! Whether you're training for competition or swimming for fitness, our top-quality facilities ensure the best experience for all.

Join us today and make a splash with Aqua Strides Academy- where passion meets the water, and every swimmer is part of the family!
